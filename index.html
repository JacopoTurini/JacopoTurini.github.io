Jacopo Turini

I'm a researcher in cognitive science and psychology, currently waiting to defend my PhD at the Goethe University of Frankfurt. My research interests have focused on the interplay of visual perception, language comprehension and semantic memory, with particular focus.

Before joining The University of Sussex, I obtain a BSc in experimental psychology at the University of Milano-Bicocca, and a MSc in Cognitive Science from the Centre of Mind and Brain Sciences (CIMeC, University of Trento), where I interned at the Reasoning and Decision Group.
